# Alerts Overview

Alerts notify you when gas reaches conditions that matter to your workflow.

## Supported alert dimensions

- Lane (`low`, `standard`, `fast`)
- Threshold comparison (`<=`, `>=`)
- Cooldown interval to suppress repeated notifications
- Delivery channel based on your account configuration

## When to use alerts

- Trigger low-fee windows for routine transactions
- Notify teams when gas spikes above operational budgets
- Feed automation pipelines that schedule execution timing
