# Usage

After setup, AI Connect can read gas data and manage alerts with your account permissions.

## Common workflows

## Check market conditions

```text
What are current low, standard, and fast gas recommendations?
```

## Create an alert

```text
Create an alert when standard gas is at or below 18 gwei and notify me by email.
```

## Review active alerts

```text
List my active gas alerts with thresholds and cooldowns.
```

## Operational guidance

- Use explicit units (`gwei`) in prompts
- Ask for confirmation before write actions in team environments
- Re-authenticate if the client reports expired consent
